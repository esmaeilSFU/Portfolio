Diamond-Wall ; A Digital Fabrication Solution

A responsive molding mechanism was developed to cast algorithmically generated geometries using a kinetic mold.

Project Overview

A digital fabrication workshop developed a responsive molding mechanism for casting algorithmically generated geometries by classifying patterns into two matrices based on generation methods and geometric properties. Customized fabrication mechanisms were designed for efficiency over universal CNC machines, ensuring integration with the original patterning method. A kinetic mold, controlled via Arduino and C# in Grasshopper, was used to produce a 200x240 cm modular wall with 120 adaptable units. Material research optimized a fast-setting concrete mix, enabling module casting in 9 minutes, with a 48-hour curing period before assembly in a steel frame on a concrete bed.
